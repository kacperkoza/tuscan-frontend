About

Tuscan.pro is an open-source project using Faceit API for presentation and analysis of recent and overall CS:GO players performance during Faceit matches. This site was created to visualize your recent and overall skill progress using colorful and intuitive graphs and charts. It was developed by players - for players.

Our code is completely open source. Feel free to contribute!
You can also check our Chrome extension.
Backend developed and maintained in Java.
We use Angular for our frontend services.
You can help us improve and remain our site ad free. Thank you!
